Mission

To support young athletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practive skills related to the game of soccer, specially those skills aroung team cooperation and good sportmanship.

Vision

The North Side Youth Soccer League aspired to develop strong, well-rounded, and mindful athletes thorugh the building of character, self-discipline, and leadership.

General Information

The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods and environment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside in a Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members
